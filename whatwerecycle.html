WHAT WE RECYCLE


Help to keep waste from growing

eXstrat specializes in the environmental friendly and socially responsible dismantling, recovery and refining of electronic waste.

All material sent to eXstrat is 100% recycled into 3 commodities: metals, plastic and glass. No Electronics are placed in landfill.

  TVs (LCD, Plasma, CRT)
  Monitors
  Computers
  Laptop
  Printers
  Audio/Video equipment
  Auto Batteries
  Copper wire
  Miscellaneous computer equipment
  Personal electronic devices
  Large Electronics (Copiers, Fax machines, Washers, Refrigerators, etc.)
  Small Electronics (Keyboards, Mouse, CD Players, Cell Phones, Telephones, etc.)
